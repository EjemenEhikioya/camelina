About Us

The name "Camelina Sunflower" is actually derived from "Camelina Reading Camp" and "Sunflower". Camelina Reading Camp focuses on improving the reading and writing skills of children. The first Camilina which was a huge success, took place in 2018 at Lagos State. This spectacular event featured colouring of pictures, question and answer section as well as educational movies like Akeelah and the Bee. Sunflower is a mobile garden school for children. Our moblie garden school services include:
1. Lessons in plant names (botanical and common names)
2. Planting seeds
3. Lessons in flowering plants and trees
4. Lessons in kitchen/edible gardens
5. Growing plants in bag and mini pots
6. Tropical flower arrangements for events and decorations
7. Lessons in plant braiding for events and decorations
